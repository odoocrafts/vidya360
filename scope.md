# CampusHive - Complete School ERP Platform
## Product Scope & Functional Specification v1.0

### Vision

CampusHive is a complete School ERP platform designed for CBSE, ICSE, State Syllabus, Private Schools, Colleges, and Educational Institutions.

The platform manages the complete student lifecycle from enquiry to alumni while connecting Management, Admissions, Teachers, Students, Parents, Accounts, HR, and Transport into a single ecosystem.
